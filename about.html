About AE-FUNAI Journal Platform

A digital repository for academic research and publications

Our Mission

The AE-FUNAI Journal Platform serves as a centralized hub for students, lecturers, and researchers to share, discover, and access academic publications. We aim to foster knowledge sharing and academic collaboration within the university community.

Features

  • Easy journal submission and review process
  • Public access to published research
  • Category-based organization
  • Search and browse functionality
  • Secure file storage and management
